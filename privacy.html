Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

1. Overview

StockPulse ("we", "our", "the app") is a client-side stock monitoring tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

2. Data We Collect

  • Watchlist symbols and alert preferences — stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Theme preference — stored locally in localStorage.
  • No account registration, no cookies, no tracking pixels.

3. Third-Party Data Sources

Live stock price data is fetched from Yahoo Finance via public CORS proxy services (corsproxy.io, allorigins.win). Your IP address may be visible to these services when requests are made. We do not control their privacy practices.

4. Third-Party Scripts

We load Chart.js and related libraries from jsDelivr CDN. All scripts are loaded with Subresource Integrity (SRI) hashes to prevent tampering. jsDelivr may log request metadata per their own privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

All data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. You can clear it at any time via your browser settings. We have no server-side storage.

6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. Clear your browser's site data to remove all stored preferences.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

8. Contact

This is an open-source demonstration project. For questions, open an issue on the project repository.
